About Me

Hello! I'm Marharyta Andreieva, a passionate designer and illustrator based in Queens, NY. I specialize in motion design and graphic design, blending visual storytelling with technical skill. Currently focusing my education on motion, I love creating engaging narrative videos using After Effects, then bringing them to life with sound and music in Premiere Pro. My background working in a print shop has also given me the ability to design quickly and efficiently in Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop, producing smart, clean visuals for both digital and print.

Highlights of my work

These are some of my graphic design projects and illustrations created over the past year. One of my largest projects was History of High Heels, a school assignment where I developed a full motion-design piece. You can watch the complete video on my YouTube channel. Among my recent works, my favorite is the Jazz Illustration—a watercolor and colored pencil piece inspired by a real bar in Williamsburg. The atmosphere of the place, along with the warmth and energy of live jazz, helped shape the details, colors, and mood of the illustration.
